About Us

Who We Are

AHM Management, LLC is the property management arm of AHM Group, a rapidly expanding real estate development company singularly focused on the City of St. Louis. Over the past five years, we've grown from a three-person operation to a team of 15 with a development pipeline of over 700 apartment units across multiple mixed-use and multi-family buildings.

We're invested in the growth of our team just as much as the growth of our portfolio — with real opportunities for leadership and advancement.

The Role

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Respond to and coordinate all tenant work orders
  • Assist in creating preventative maintenance plans (HVAC filters, smoke detectors, roof inspections, mechanical inspections)
  • Diagnose and perform minor and routine maintenance repairs in a timely, professional manner
  • Inspect vacant units between move-out and move-in
  • Manage tool and material inventories
  • Diagnose and fix physical building issues using tools and equipment
  • Available for on-call needs as required
What We're Looking For

Qualifications

  • 2–5 years of property maintenance experience (at least 1 year in multi-family) — preferred
  • Experience with building trades preferred
  • Basic knowledge of electrical, plumbing, HVAC/mechanical systems, and painting
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Comfort working non-standard hours
  • Familiarity with building standards and municipal/state regulatory codes
  • Ability to adapt to a fluid, fast-paced daily routine
  • CPO Certification recommended
  • EPA Certification recommended
  • Valid driver's license required
  • Must be able to commute to or relocate to St. Louis, MO 63103
